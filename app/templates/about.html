ClientHuntr was developed as part of an Insight Data Science project, to help a large pharmaceutical company explore their customer base.

Customer and item information are anonymized as customer code and item number, but the customers are small-to-medium pharmacies and the items correspond to generic over-the-counter and prescription medications.
